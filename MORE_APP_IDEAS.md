## TIER 1: BUILD NEXT (Highest Signal)

### 1. **CRM Voice Commander**
**Signal:** 9.5/10 - Eliminates friction in your #1 money-making activity
**Problem:** Updating Notion CRM after every customer interaction is tedious → you skip it → follow-ups fall through cracks
**Solution:** Voice-first CRM updates
- "crm check" → See all today's follow-ups instantly
- "crm done John Smith" → Marks contacted, advances step, calculates next date
- "crm hot Sarah Johnson" → Creates urgent follow-up flag
- Works from phone lock screen (Siri Shortcut integration)
**Impact:** 20-30% more consistent follow-ups = $10K-30K more annual sales
**URL:** m3rcey.github.io/crm-voice/

### 2. **Shift Performance Tracker**
**Signal:** 9/10 - Makes you a better salesperson faster
**Problem:** You don't know which behaviors drive your best days
**Solution:** Log each customer interaction with outcome
- Log: greeting approach, needs assessment, add-ons offered, close attempt
- Track: ticket size, close rate, add-on rate by approach type
- Insights: "You close 40% higher when you mention adjustable bases first"
**Impact:** Compress 2 years of sales learning into 6 months
**URL:** m3rcey.github.io/shift-tracker/

### 3. **QullaBot Trade Journal**
**Signal:** 8.5/10 - Paper trading → real trading faster
**Problem:** You're placing trades but not systematically reviewing what works
**Solution:** Trade logging with Qullamaggie-specific pattern recognition
- Log: ticker, setup type, star rating, entry, stop, exit
- Auto-calculate: R-multiple, expectancy, win rate by setup type
- Pattern alerts: "You've lost last 3 parabolic shorts - review required"
**Impact:** Avoid repeating costly mistakes, double down on winning patterns
**URL:** m3rcey.github.io/trade-journal/
