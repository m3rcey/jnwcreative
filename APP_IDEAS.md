## Tier 1: HIGHEST SIGNAL (Build First)

### 1. **Quote-to-Close Calculator** 
**Signal:** 9/10 - Directly impacts sales conversions
**Problem:** You're calculating financing, monthly payments, and savings in your head during sales conversations
**Solution:** Quick calculator showing:
- Monthly payment breakdown (0% APR, 12-mo, 24-mo, 36-mo)
- Sleep improvement ROI (better sleep = better life value)
- Comparison vs competitor pricing
- Instant financing qualification estimate
**Impact:** Faster closes, higher ticket averages, more confident presentations
**URL:** m3rcey.github.io/quote-closer/

### 2. **Follow-Up Fire Alarm**
**Signal:** 9/10 - Prevents lost sales
**Problem:** Hot prospects go cold because follow-ups slip through cracks
**Solution:** Voice-triggered urgency detector:
- "crm fire" = Find all Hot prospects not contacted in 48+ hours
- Shows: prospect name, quote amount, days since contact, suggested message
- One-tap call/SMS/email from the app
**Impact:** Recover 10-20% more sales from warm leads
**URL:** m3rcey.github.io/followup-fire/

### 3. **QullaBot Trade Journal**
**Signal:** 8.5/10 - Accelerates trading mastery
**Problem:** You're paper trading but not systematically reviewing mistakes
**Solution:** Trade logging with Qullamaggie-specific analysis:
- Log entry: ticker, setup type (breakout/EP/parabolic/mean-reversion), star rating
- Auto-calculate: R-multiple, days held, exit reason
- Pattern recognition: "You succeed on 5-star breakouts but lose on 3-star"
- Weekly review summary
**Impact:** Compress 2 years of learning into 6 months
**URL:** m3rcey.github.io/trade-journal/
