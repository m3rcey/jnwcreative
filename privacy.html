Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Gateway Grocery Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Gateway Grocery Assistant website and mobile application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your name and email address.
  • Shopping List Data: Items you add to your cart or shopping list, including product names, quantities, and preferences.
  • Search Queries: Terms you enter when searching for products.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the Service (pages visited, features used).
  • Device Data: Basic device information such as browser type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Generate shopping lists and carts based on your input
  • Connect you with our checkout partners to complete orders
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you updates and notifications related to the Service
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Checkout Partners: When you complete a purchase, we share necessary order information (items, quantities, delivery address) with our trusted partners (such as Instacart) to generate a cart or checkout link. All payment processing happens directly on the partner's platform.
  • Service Providers: With vendors who host the Service, provide technical infrastructure, or assist with analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.

4. Partner Checkout

Gateway Grocery Assistant is a list-building tool. When you proceed to checkout, you are redirected to a trusted third-party partner (such as Instacart) to complete your order. This includes:

  • Providing payment information directly to the partner
  • Selecting delivery or pickup options offered by the partner
  • Receiving order confirmations and support from the partner

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of our checkout partners. Please review their privacy policies separately.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We do not use cookies or third-party analytics by default. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy and provide clear notice.

Our checkout partners may use their own cookies and tracking technologies. We do not control their practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active, and for up to 30 days after account deletion.
  • Shopping List Data: Retained for up to 90 days after your last interaction, unless you request earlier deletion.
  • Analytics Data: Retained in anonymized form for up to 12 months.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data. We will process such requests within 30 days.
  • Opt-Out: You can delete your account at any time by contacting us at Joshn.wms@gmail.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
