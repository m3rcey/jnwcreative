How It Works

Search for items

Type what you need — from fresh produce to household staples. Our search covers major retailers.

Build your list

Add items to your cart or keep a running list. Review quantities and make changes anytime.

Connect to checkout

When ready, we'll connect you to a trusted partner to complete your order securely.

Pick up or get delivered

Choose pickup from a nearby store or delivery to your door. Availability depends on partner coverage.

Our Commitment

We are a solo developer project based in St. Louis, Missouri. We are not affiliated with any retailers.

  • We do not sell groceries — we help you build a list and connect you to trusted checkout partners.
  • Delivery and pickup availability depends on our partners' coverage in your area.
  • We value your privacy and are transparent about what data we collect.
